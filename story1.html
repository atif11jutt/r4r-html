Winner of the German Design Award 2022!

November 2021

Schmid+Wolf is very pleased about the "Special Mention" award for the corporate design of Calingo Insurance AG in the category - Excellent Communications Design - Corporate Identity

The German Design Award (GDA) is the international premium prize of the German Design Council. The German Design Council is one of the world's leading competence centers for communication and brand management in the field of design. His goal: to discover, present and award unique design trends. Every year, top-class submissions from product and communication design are awarded, all of which are groundbreaking in the international design landscape in their own way. The German Design Award, initiated in 2012, is one of the most recognized design competitions worldwide and enjoys a high reputation far beyond specialist circles.
